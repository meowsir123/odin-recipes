Lasagna

Description

The humble lasagna is an Italian dish made from layering pieces of pasta with cheese and tomato sauce and then baking them until golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Italian sausage
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 28oz. can crushed tomatoes
  • 3 oz. tomato paste (about 5 Tbsp)
  • 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 2 cups whole milk ricotta
  • 1 cup shredded Italian cheese blend
